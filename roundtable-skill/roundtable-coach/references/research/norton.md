# Jerry Norton — Flipping & Wholesaling Deals (Researched Dossier)

> Research correction: Norton's signature deal formula is the **65% formula** (not the generic "70% rule").

## Credentials
- Founder of Flipping Mastery (2014) — training + software (Flipster) for wholesalers, flippers, and agents; investing since 2004 with thousands of deals; among the largest flipping-education YouTube/podcast audiences in the country; flagship Crash Course Wholesaling (200+ minutes of training, Deal Analyzer, sample contracts, "90 ways to find motivated sellers in any market").

## The 65% Formula (verified, verbatim)
- **"ARV (after repair value) × .65 — repairs = buy price."** The 35% held back covers profit + closing costs + carrying costs, targeting ~20% profit margins.
- Worked example (his): $200,000 ARV with $30,000 repairs → $200,000 × 0.65 = $130,000 − $30,000 = **$100,000 buy price**.
- Discipline doctrine: hold the 65% line REGARDLESS of market conditions — the formula is the business.

## Market-Timing Insight (his contrarian edge)
- In hot seller's markets most investors abandon discipline and pay at 75% — that gap IS the wholesaler's opportunity: the disciplined buyer at $100K wholesales to the 75%-buyer at ~$120K and keeps ~$20K.
- Fee inflation he cites: wholesale fees rose from historical ~$5,000 averages to $20,000-$30,000 ranges in favorable conditions.

## Success Methods (his big three)
1. Get BETTER at finding deals (the whole game is acquisition).
2. Improve marketing.
3. Make MORE offers.

## Deal-Finding Arsenal (his "90 ways" categories)
- Driving for dollars (distress cues → skip trace), probate and pre-foreclosure lists, tax delinquents, absentee owners, code violations, agent referrals for as-is listings; Flipster software automates comps/offers/lead flow.

## Exit-Strategy Stack (his multi-exit underwriting)
- Underwrite EVERY lead against multiple exits before offering: wholesale (assignment), fix-and-flip, or WHOLETAIL (light clean-up, sell as-is on MLS) — pick the exit AFTER you know the discount depth. For wholesaling, subtract your assignment fee from the MAO.

## Working With Sellers
- Solve the PROBLEM (speed, certainty, as-is, leave-what-you-want) and the price follows; repair-estimate triage by $/sqft tiers (cosmetic/medium/gut) to move fast, line-item only when serious.

## What He Argues Against
- Chasing appreciation instead of buying discount ("the money is made at acquisition").
- Breaking formula in hot markets (that's how flippers go broke at the peak).
- One-exit underwriting.
- Analysis paralysis — offers are the metric, not spreadsheets.
