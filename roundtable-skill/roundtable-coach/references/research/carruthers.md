# Ricky Carruth — Cold Calling & Relationships-Over-Transactions (Researched Dossier)

> Research correction: the correct name is **Ricky Carruth** (Zero to Diamond), not "Carruthers."

## Credentials
- Gulf Shores, Alabama solo agent: 100+ residential transactions per year while coaching; founder of **Zero to Diamond**, a FREE coaching company whose stated mission is "to reduce the failure rate in the real estate industry by bringing awareness that it's not about the deal, it's about the relationship." Author of "Zero to Diamond: Become a Million Dollar Real Estate Agent."

## Core Philosophy
- **Relationships over transactions** — the brand's one-line creed. Value people over individual deals and you close MORE deals long-term. "The agent who connects with the most prospects will ultimately win."
- The sailing-boat metaphor: the business is "hard to start but easier to maintain once in motion" — consistency compounds into countless future deals.
- Listings first: obtain listings as the foundation; buyers naturally follow.
- Time doctrine: "Time is money" — but time is MORE valuable than money since it can't be regained; success is "knowing how to allocate time to maximize results."

## Named Frameworks
- **The Five-Step Closing Process**: (1) Find prospects → (2) Follow up via call/appointment/relationship → (3) Convert to client contract → (4) Negotiate → (5) Process and close.
- **The FSBO free-help play** (verbatim): "I want to help you for free." When they ask why: **"Why wouldn't I?"** — disarms the pitch-expectation completely; the relationship earns the listing when self-selling stalls.
- **Mail + call stacking**: mail-outs alone convert ~3%; mail-outs FOLLOWED by calls convert ~18% — the follow-up call is the multiplier, not the mail.
- **The 90-Day Action Plan**: his structured onboarding — relationship-first daily actions; e.g., five minutes daily calling one past client = 350+ relationship touches a year, compounding repeat/referral business.
- **Calling cadence**: 20-30 calls, 2-3 days weekly minimum (solo-agent sustainable volume); distraction-free environment; ALWAYS leave voicemails; every call has a prepared reason (recent sale, market update).
- **Daily organization**: two 15-minute documentation sessions (morning + afternoon); written goals; prioritize income-generating activities and never fixate on one stalled deal.
- **Referral engine**: ask "WHO do you know..." (names, not yes/no); actively ask through 60 days post-closing; reputation built on kept promises, availability, researched answers, high energy.

## Communication Style
- Open simply: "How are you doing today?" before business. 80/20 rule — listen more than talk. Comfort in the first few seconds decides the call.
- 90% of the business still comes from traditional contact (mail, calls) versus technology — tools change, conversations close.
- Prospect data craft: reverse phone lookup + county tax records.

## What He Argues Against
- Transaction-first, commission-breath selling (the industry's failure rate is his enemy).
- Paid-lead dependence — conversations with owners are free.
- Quitting during the hard-start phase of the boat.
- High-pressure FSBO/expired approaches — free help beats clever closes.
