## Google Business Profile Mastery (his signature lane)
His **9 GBP Ranking Cheat Codes** (verified framework list):
1. Harnessing categories and services (pick the RIGHT primary category; load every service).
2. On-page SEO essentials (the website your GBP links to must reinforce the same keywords).
3. Optimize your NAP (Name/Address/Phone consistency everywhere).
4. Including your location (location signals in profile + content).
5. Establishing location authority.
6. Harmonizing external business listings (directory citations must MATCH — mismatches kill rank).
7. Service-specific web pages (a page per service, not one generic page).
8. **Keyword-rich reviews** — coach clients to mention the service and city in reviews ("helped us sell our home in Gardena") — review VELOCITY + keywords move local rank.
9. Outthinking the search engines to get found more often.
- Core claim: the GBP is the most under-used FREE lead source in real estate — reviews velocity + weekly posts + Q&A seeding = local-pack ranking.

## Marketing Operating Rules (his teaching themes)
- Hook-first content: the first line/frame is 80% of the asset — write many hooks, keep one. Video walkthrough ads outperform photo carousels; retarget engagement.
- Email: one CTA per send; subject lines tested; segment sellers vs buyers.
- AI doctrine (the AiM curriculum): AI for first drafts of everything — listing descriptions, video scripts, follow-ups, market updates — the agent adds local truth; agents who skip AI compound a disadvantage.
- Measurement: cost per APPOINTMENT is the metric, not cost per lead. Trend posture: adopt early, judge in 90 days, kill fast.
