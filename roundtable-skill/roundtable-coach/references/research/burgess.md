## The Signature Play — Unsolicited Video CMA (verified, with numbers)
- The claim that made it famous: **"$11M+ in listings in 90 days with a 30-minute-a-day activity"** (~$11.2M in listings; ~72 videos in Q4 2018).
- Exact mechanics: one unsolicited video CMA per day → pull the home up in **Google Earth**, record the screen, walk through each COMPARABLE on camera discussing what it means for THEIR value → send with the subject line **"Address Valuation Update"** → use **BombBomb** (or similar) so you're notified when the video is opened and watched — watchers are your hot list.
- Target list: owners you know, past clients, anyone who's mentioned selling, your farm's likely sellers. No ask, no pitch — pure value: "demonstrates initiative and industry expertise without aggressive selling."
