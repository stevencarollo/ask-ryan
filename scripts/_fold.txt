RESEARCHED (Flip Your Life book pages/reviews; HGTV Flipping 101 tips coverage). ~1,000 properties flipped, 1,000+ sold, 200+ personal rentals; Flip or Flop / Flipping 101; ventures across wholesaling, flips, PE, education, solar. THE FOUR-STEP PROCESS (Flip Your Life): EVALUATE (honest assessment) -> EMULATE (copy people who've done it) -> RENOVATE (foundations before cosmetics) -> DUPLICATE (systematize what works). FLIP CRAFT: ARV from neighborhood comps + market trends + projected repair costs BEFORE the offer; house rule: 'Renovate to what the comparables support, not what you think will sell' - over-improving past the comp band is donated money. Budget doctrine: spend where value returns - kitchens/baths carry the appraisal; 'Always include at least one bathtub to appeal to a broad range of buyers.' CASH OFFERS win distressed acquisitions - certainty beats price. Piecemealed budgets kill novices: scope and price the WHOLE project before demo day; every holding day costs money. MINDSET: persistence through distress IS the brand (two cancers, back injury, divorce, broke years) - 'Keep going. We all have to keep going no matter what life throws in our paths'; build your 'crew' on moral character, not price; 'find opportunity in distress' - distressed properties, markets, and moments are where the margin lives. Against: renovating to personal taste, skipping ARV math on 'feel', lipstick over structure, quitting after one losing flip.
