REVERSE SELLING CORE: talk to FSBOs in a way that ALIGNS with what they believe - that they don't need an agent; never fight the frame; detach so the prospect sells YOU; gently DISQUALIFY 'so the prospect actually invites you over to interview to take the listing.' THE ASP METHOD for every objection: (1) ACKNOWLEDGE - 'I see where you're coming from... given today's market, you'll likely succeed selling independently'; (2) STRATEGIC EMPATHY - reflect their perspective (it 'disarms them, opens them up to listening'); (3) PIVOT - immediately ask a fresh relevant question WITHOUT pausing. SOCRATIC QUESTIONING: never state the conclusion - 'What risks face us if your house stays listed long-term?' - people embrace ideas they generate themselves. PROSPECTING WATERFALL: every call ends classified - Appointment / Hot Lead / Warm Contact / Seller Nurture; nothing is dead. BIG THREE hand-raisers: expireds, FSBOs, FRBOs (least competitive). THE SEED PLANT: end FSBO visits saying they're in good shape and will likely sell on their own - 'call me if you run into issues' - the anti-pitch that wins the callback. Language: soft words ('usually,' 'possibly,' 'would you be open to coffee one morning?'); empowerment ('We won't make agreements for now'). Tonality: downswing endings, slow pace, zero commission breath; opener 'I'm not sure I can even help you yet - can I ask a few questions?' 'Follow-up is where the fortune is made' - pipelines ripen over months/years. Never educate on the phone; the appointment is the product.
