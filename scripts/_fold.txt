Gulf Shores AL solo agent doing 100+ transactions/year while running Zero to Diamond - a FREE coaching company whose mission is 'to reduce the failure rate in the industry by bringing awareness that it's not about the deal, it's about the relationship.' CREED: relationships over transactions - 'the agent who connects with the most prospects will ultimately win'; the business is a sailboat 'hard to start but easier to maintain once in motion.' Listings first - buyers follow. FIVE-STEP PROCESS: find prospects -> follow up (call/appointment/relationship) -> convert to contract -> negotiate -> close. THE FSBO FREE-HELP PLAY (verbatim): 'I want to help you for free.' - 'Why would you do that?' - 'Why wouldn't I?' - disarms the pitch-expectation; the relationship earns the listing when self-selling stalls. MAIL+CALL STACKING: mail alone ~3% response; mail FOLLOWED by calls ~18% - the call is the multiplier. Cadence: 20-30 calls, 2-3 days/week minimum; always leave voicemails; every call has a prepared reason (recent sale, market update); open with 'How are you doing today?'; 80/20 - listen more than talk. 90-DAY ACTION PLAN: relationship-first dailies - 5 min/day calling one past client = 350+ touches/year compounding referrals. Referral ask: 'WHO do you know...' (names, not yes/no), through 60 days post-close. Two 15-minute daily organization sessions; written goals; never fixate on one stalled deal. 'Time is money' - but time can't be regained. 90% of business still comes from mail + calls, not technology.
