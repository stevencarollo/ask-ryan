RESEARCHED (Denis D. Medium long-form takeaways; Shortform summary; HousingWire review; serhant.com). CEO of SERHANT., MDLNY/Owning Manhattan, author Sell It Like Serhant / Big Money Energy / Brand It Like Serhant. THE THREE F's: Follow-up (send IMMEDIATELY after connecting), Follow-through (said it = do it; update clients through the whole process), Follow-back (reconnect past clients on rhythm). FINDER/KEEPER/DOER: allocate daily time across all three roles - Finder (new business), Keeper (finance/strategy), Doer (execution); all-Doer agents starve their pipeline. BALL JUGGLING: 'keep more balls in the air' - many deals at once kills desperation; SIX REASONS BALLS DROP: (1) failure to communicate, (2) REPLYING instead of responding, (3) unrealistic expectations, (4) lacking knowledge, (5) wrong approach for that client, (6) focusing on the money. THE FOUR W's: Why (purpose), Work (actions), Wall (what you're running FROM - leverage the fear), Win (what success means). SEVEN CLIENT STAGES: Excitement -> Frustration -> Fear -> Disappointment -> Acceptance -> Happiness -> Relief - manage the STAGE, not just the deal. WOW EFFECT: show the premium option FIRST to anchor, then the middle option closes. 'Build the house first' - clients and revenue before office and logo. Daily quota: 3+ NEW contacts every day ('you are surrounded by opportunity'). 'Your day should start a day before' - plan the night prior, hardest task first. PSYCHOLOGY: 'People don't like being sold. They like to shop with friends'; people buy emotionally - sell the improved LIFE, not square footage; objections in nearly every deal - agree, reframe, never defend; face-to-face closes; say YES to uncomfortable opportunities. Brand: video before comfortable, hook-first, consistency + authenticity + visibility + value.
