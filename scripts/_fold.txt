Make money when you buy. Build the Core Four BEFORE the deal. Systems make distance irrelevant.
RESEARCHED (Old Dawgs REI ep585 detailed notes; BiggerPockets BRRRR guide/book pages). Ex-BiggerPockets podcast co-host; author of the BRRRR book + Long-Distance Real Estate Investing (NAREE award runner-up). BRRRR (verified worked example): buy distressed $60K -> rehab $30K -> $90K total in -> ARV $120K -> refi at 75% LTV = $90K loan -> ALL capital recovered -> repeat. DOCTRINE: 'make money when you buy' - purchase at 75% OR LESS of ARV or the math dies; underwrite the REFI APPRAISAL before buying; verify lender seasoning; hold reserves. THE CORE FOUR (built BEFORE the deal): deal-finding agent, property manager, local lender, contractor - 'you must rely on other people'; hack: call a big brokerage's office manager, ask 'who is your best agent for distressed housing?' and have that agent refer the other three. MARKETS: 1% screen ($100K should rent ~$1K/mo) then REAL math (PITI + vacancy + repairs + CapEx + management); coastal swings = buy recessions for appreciation, Midwest/South = BRRRR country; 'Don't try to find the next Hot Market - just look for a market where BRRRR will work!' THREE FORMS OF DISTRESS to target: market, personal (divorce/medical/debt), property (optimal for BRRRR); MLS play: 90-150 days-on-market; agent play: listings at <=60% of area average price. THREE BUY REQUIREMENTS: under-market, must cash flow after ALL expenses, safe neighborhood. CONTRACTOR CONTROLS: never pay upfront, itemized scope contract, first draw only, PM/agent verifies before each next draw. 'Get rich slow.'
