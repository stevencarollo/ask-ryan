RESEARCHED (Carrot long-form strategy piece; subto.com first-deal guide; OfferMarket/Levine profiles; Wealth without Cash). A&E Triple Digit Flip host; 1,000+ doors / ~$150M portfolio built on creative finance; SubTo community founder. CORE (verbatim): 'You have to either get better or get creative' - when you can't win on price or credit, win on STRUCTURE; carry MANY tools and match the tool to the SELLER'S problem. STRUCTURES: SUBJECT-TO - buyer takes deed, seller's mortgage stays in place, buyer pays it (loan NOT assumed; for debt-relief + speed sellers; respect due-on-sale honestly, hold reserves). SELLER FINANCE - the free-and-clear play; negotiate PRICE OR TERMS, never both; verified example: $130,000 property at $0 down, 0% interest, $350/month - renting at $1,600/month (full price bought perfect terms). HYBRID - sub-to the existing loan + seller carries the equity as a note. NOVATION - renovate/list on the seller's title, split upside above a guaranteed floor. Win logic: seller nets more (full price, as-is, no showings); buyer skips banks/appraisals. NUMBERS: converts ~1 in 22 leads - 'double and even triple' the market rate; ~10-12 wholesale + 10-12 creative deals/month. SOURCING: texts, wholesalers/agents (top source - network via FB groups/Meetups), door knocking, probate attorneys, and OTHER INVESTORS' DEAD LEADS (no-equity = creative gold). Conversation: the barrier is fear of calling - teach via LIVE calls; explain structures through deal STORIES not jargon. Paperwork: title company/attorney closes, seller offered independent counsel, third-party loan servicing. Against: 'you need cash and credit', one-tool price-bidding, hiding the ball.
