RESEARCHED (PropStream Pros profile; REDX live-scripts feature; Realtyna interview; loidavelasquez.com). Real Broker agent, CA+FL, built from scratch (first listing Nov 2015) purely on cold calls, doors, expireds & FSBOs; 95K+ YouTube subs / 5M views of no-fluff prospecting training; bilingual EN/ES - serves Spanish-speaking clients and trains Spanish-first agents; hosts New In Real Estate Podcast. DOCTRINE: expireds/FSBOs are 'the lowest hanging fruit' - owners already raised their hand; most agents avoid them out of rejection fear. THE 30-CONVERSATION STANDARD: 30 real conversations daily, held through rejection streaks - a signature deal came on call #30 after a day of nos. DISCOVERY BEFORE PITCH: open with 'What happened?' / 'Why were you trying to sell?'; key read: 'The more frustrated someone is over the phone, it's probably because they were very motivated to sell.' Prepare 4-5 RESPONSES PER OBJECTION, drilled via role-play with agents from other regions (different personalities/styles). VERBATIM expired opener: 'Hi Rick! My name is Loida and I'm a local real estate agent. I'm calling about your home on 123 Main St. I noticed it recently came off the market as an expired. When will you start interviewing agents to get it sold?' -> 'Did you get any showings?' -> 'So if the home sold in less than 30 days, that wouldn't be an issue for you, right?' (assumptive interview question, showings diagnosis, assumptive trial close). Day structure: prospect mornings, follow-ups/appointments afternoons, videos in between. Content rule: 'The best videos come from explaining the things you already know'; optimize WATCH TIME not likes; took ~3 years of weekly videos before growth exploded. Emotional control: 'I can only control how I respond.'
