How It Works

Three moves to better answers.

I

Select your advisors

Check the seats you want filled. A focused panel of eight or fewer unlocks each advisor's full playbook — frameworks, formulas, and scripts.

II

Ask, or hand over a file

Type your situation — or upload a prelim, contract, lead list, flyer, headshot, even a scanned PDF. The panel reads every page.

III

Talk it through

Your coach answers, asks the next sharp question, and remembers the whole thread. Reply, push back, go deeper — like a real session.
