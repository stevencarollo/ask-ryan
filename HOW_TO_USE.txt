1. CLICK in the text box
   └─ Where it says "What's your real estate challenge?"

2. TYPE your question
   └─ Example: "How do I overcome price objections?"
   └─ Be specific: "Client keeps hesitating at closing, what do I say?"

   ├─ Ranked citations with sources
   ├─ Confidence score
   └─ Optional audio playback

💡 PRO TIPS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

✓ Be specific in your question
  GOOD:  "Client balked at the $500k asking price, what's my comeback?"
  BAD:   "How do I sell?"

✓ Describe the actual situation
  GOOD:  "Buyer has financing issues and won't commit"
  BAD:   "What about buyers?"

✓ Ask for scripts if you want exact language
  GOOD:  "What should I say when a buyer says this is overpriced?"
  BAD:   "Tell me about pricing"

✓ Try different response styles
  • Practical = scripts you use today
  • Motivational = build confidence
  • Technical = understand the psychology
